Hockey Stick Buyer's Guide


Which stick is right for me?

It depends, if you've had a lot of experiance in the past it's probably better to get a nice stick. On the flip side, if you've never played hockey before it's best to stay safe and spend less money. If your in the midde, we reccommend you buy a stick in the range of 30% to 70% carbon.


Beginner Sticks (0% to 30% carbon)

For people just starting.


Intermediate Sticks (30% to 70% carbon)

For people that play a bit and are pretty good.


Expert Sticks (70% to 100% carbon)

For professional players who play county and up.
