Y1 ADB 30, 50 and 70

Adidas ChaosFury Kromaskin .1

Y1 YLB 50 and 70

The Y1 YLB 70 hockey stick is crafted with 70% premium Japanese carbon and 30% fibreglass/aramid, providing a great balance of power and control. Weighing 530g, it features an aggressive low bow and Carbon Power Chain technology for increased stiffness and performance. The stick's natural concave shape aids in drag flicking, while the enhanced backhand zone ensures powerful hits. This makes it an excellent choice for intermediate players looking to improve their game.
